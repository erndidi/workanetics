The import button lies

What actually breaks in a migration

Built-in import tools handle the easy 80%. These are the parts that quietly go missing — and the reason a migration deserves an engineer, not an import button.

Open AR balances

Partial payments, adjustments, and credits that don't map cleanly between systems — the money that disappears without anyone noticing until month-end.

Claims & denial history

Years of adjudication history that appeals, audits, and timely-filing disputes depend on. Import tools routinely truncate or drop it.

Payer & fee schedules

Payer IDs, contracts, and fee schedules that don't line up field-for-field, causing rejections for weeks after go-live.

Custom fields & documents

Attached EOBs, notes, and the custom fields your workflow secretly depends on — the data no standard mapping knows exists.
